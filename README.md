<h1 align="center">AgriSmart AI</h1>

<p align="center">
AI-Powered Smart Farming Assistant for Modern Agriculture
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Project Overview</h2>

<p>
AgriSmart AI is an intelligent agriculture assistance platform designed to empower farmers through Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Voice Technology, Weather Analytics, and Regional Language Support. The platform helps farmers make informed decisions, improve crop productivity, reduce losses, and adopt sustainable farming practices.
</p>

<p>
The solution is specifically designed to bridge the technology gap in rural agriculture by providing easy-to-use interfaces, multilingual support, and AI-driven recommendations accessible through both text and voice interactions. AgriSmart AI aims to make advanced agricultural knowledge available to every farmer regardless of technical expertise or literacy level.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Core Features</h2>

<h3>1. AI-Powered Agricultural Advisory Assistant</h3>

<p>
The platform includes an intelligent agricultural assistant capable of answering farming-related queries in real time. Farmers receive crop-specific recommendations, fertilizer suggestions, irrigation guidance, pest management advice, and best farming practices based on their crop type, location, season, and farming conditions.
</p>

<h3>2. AI Crop Quality Scan & Disease Detection</h3>

<p>
Using Computer Vision and Deep Learning technologies, farmers can upload images of crops to identify diseases, pest infestations, nutrient deficiencies, and other crop health issues. The system also performs crop quality grading, harvest readiness assessment, and provides recommendations to improve crop yield and quality.
</p>

<h3>3. Voice-Based Farmer Assistant</h3>

<p>
To support farmers with limited literacy and technical knowledge, AgriSmart AI provides voice-enabled interaction. Farmers can ask questions through speech, while the system converts voice inputs into actionable queries and delivers responses in audio format, creating an inclusive and user-friendly experience.
</p>

<h3>4. Regional Language Translation System</h3>

<p>
The platform supports multiple Indian languages, enabling farmers to access agricultural guidance in their preferred language. Recommendations, disease diagnoses, weather alerts, and AI-generated insights are automatically translated into regional languages, improving accessibility and understanding.
</p>

<h3>5. Smart Weather & Farm Health Dashboard</h3>

<p>
The dashboard provides real-time weather information, rainfall forecasts, temperature trends, humidity analysis, and weather-based farming alerts. It also generates a Farm Health Score based on crop conditions, farming activities, and environmental factors, helping farmers make better operational decisions.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>AI Integration</h2>

<p>
AgriSmart AI leverages Artificial Intelligence to provide intelligent, personalized, and actionable agricultural insights. Natural Language Processing powers the Agricultural Advisory Assistant, while Computer Vision models enable crop disease detection and crop quality analysis. Machine Learning algorithms generate recommendations based on environmental conditions, farming history, weather patterns, and crop-specific requirements.
</p>

<p>
The platform continuously analyzes agricultural data to identify potential risks, improve productivity, reduce crop losses, and support proactive decision-making for farmers.
</p>
